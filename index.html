How to Make Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

Recipe

Ingredients
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 Tbsp applesauce

Directions

Step 1
Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread, and applesauce on the other slice. Place them together, cut in half, and serve.
